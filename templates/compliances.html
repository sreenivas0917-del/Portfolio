üîê Data Privacy & Cybersecurity

Protecting customer and client data is central to our operations. We maintain a layered security posture combining people, process and technology to minimize risk.

  • Encryption: 256-bit encryption for data-at-rest and TLS 1.2+ for data-in-transit.
  • Access Controls: Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) and periodic access reviews.
  • Monitoring: 24/7 SIEM monitoring, log retention & anomaly detection.
  • Endpoint Security: Managed EDR and secure configurations for remote work.

We run quarterly vulnerability scans and annual penetration tests by accredited third parties. All employees sign NDAs and complete mandatory privacy & security training during onboarding and annually thereafter.

üìò Legal & Regulatory Compliance

SynergiPro aligns its operational policies with applicable laws and industry guidance. Our compliance areas include:

  • RBI Guidelines: Collection & recovery practices, fair treatment of borrowers and grievance redressal.
  • Indian Statutes: IT Act, Companies Act, labor laws (PF/ESI), and tax regulations.
  • Financial Data Handling: PCI-DSS best practices where payment data is in scope.
  • Cross-border Processing: Data transfer agreements and minimal data transfer principles for international operations.

We maintain an internal compliance register, perform periodic compliance audits, and publish annual compliance attestations for clients and partners upon request.

üìú Certifications & Standards

Our systems and processes are independently certified or mapped against internationally recognized standards:

  • ISO 9001: Quality Management
  • ISO/IEC 27001: Information Security Management
  • PCI-DSS: Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards (where applicable)
  • GDPR Principles: Data subject rights and lawful processing for EU residents

Certifications are renewably audited. Clients may request copies of our certification attestations under NDA.

üë• Employee Conduct & Workplace Ethics
