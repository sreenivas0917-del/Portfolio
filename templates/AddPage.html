Structured Collection Process (DPD-based)

A compliant, stage-wise approach with measurable outcomes and escalation controls.

DPD 0–30 • Early Engagement

Automated reminders, soft calls, and payment facilitation to prevent slippage. Highest customer experience focus.

Outcome: High promise-to-pay rate; reduced escalation

DPD 31–90 • Negotiation Phase

Account segmentation, tailored settlement offers and supervisory approvals for structured recoveries.

Outcome: Maximized recoveries with minimal legal cost

DPD 91–180 • Intensified Recovery

Field visits, legal notices, and intensive follow-ups with compliance logs and client approvals.

Outcome: Balanced recovery vs. legal overhead

DPD 181+ • Legal & Closure

Legal escalation, auction coordination, settlements & final closure documentation with client reporting.

Outcome: Final resolution, asset realization & compliance-certified closure
