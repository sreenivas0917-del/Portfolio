Our End-to-End Recovery Process

Our structured, compliant and tech-enabled process ensures timely and ethical recovery without compromising client reputation.

  1. Account Verification: Detailed analysis of loan agreements, outstanding amounts, customer contact validation.
  2. Soft Recovery (0-30 days PAR): SMS/Email reminders, IVR calls, UPI-based payment links.
  3. Field Recovery (30-90 days PAR): Trained field executives visit borrower, counsel & negotiate repayment.
  4. Legal Action (90+ DPD): Legal notices, arbitration, court case filing, SARFAESI proceedings if required.
  5. Closure & Reporting: Payment collection, NOC issuance, legal withdrawal & MIS report submission.
