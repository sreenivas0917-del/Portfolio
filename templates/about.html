About Us

Building trust, ensuring compliance, and empowering recovery with professionalism.

At SynergiPro, we specialize in ethical and efficient loan recovery services, helping financial institutions, NBFCs, fintech companies, and businesses recover overdue payments while protecting customer dignity. With years of expertise in handling collections across DPD 0 to 365+, we understand the importance of maintaining strong client relationships while ensuring timely recovery of funds.

We operate under strict compliance with the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) guidelines, ensuring that our recovery processes are transparent, professional, and legally sound. Our focus lies in striking the right balance between firm recovery and customer sensitivity, safeguarding both lender interests and borrower rights.

Our Mission

To redefine the recovery and collection ecosystem in India by offering technology-driven, compliant, and customer-friendly solutions. We aim to help lenders minimize non-performing assets (NPAs) while empowering borrowers to improve their financial health and rebuild their creditworthiness.

Our Vision

To become India’s most trusted recovery partner by leveraging innovation, compliance, and people-first strategies, ensuring a win-win situation for both lenders and borrowers. We envision a financial ecosystem where recovery processes are no longer seen as intimidating but as structured pathways toward resolution and growth.

Our Approach

We believe recovery is not just about collecting dues but about rebuilding financial trust. Our approach includes:

  • Preventive Strategies: Early reminders, customer education, and soft collections to avoid slippages.
  • Structured Recovery: Segmenting portfolios based on DPD (Days Past Due) to apply the right strategy at the right time.
  • Technology-Driven Insights: AI-powered tools and predictive models for efficient recovery cycles.
  • Compliance First: Ensuring all recovery activities adhere to RBI and legal frameworks.
  • Customer-Centric: Maintaining borrower dignity, offering settlement options, and assisting in credit score rebuilding.

At SynergiPro, we don’t just recover dues—we restore confidence in the financial system. By combining professionalism, technology, and compassion, we are transforming the way debt recovery is managed in India.
